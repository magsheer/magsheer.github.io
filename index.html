About

Hi, I work as a Software Engineer at Motorola Solutions, Inc. For the past couple of years I’ve called the sunny Bay Area home. I earned my Master’s in Computer Science from the University of Utah. Before that, I worked as a System Software Engineer at Hewlett Packard Enterprise in Bengaluru, India. You can find my resume here.

When I am not working, you can find me trying a new recipe, sewing or crocheting (knitting never stuck). I often dream of building a fully me-made wardrobe, even if it still feels a bit out of reach. I love paddle boarding on the bay, practicing yoga, and tackling an ever-growing list of side projects. I’m also always planning my next adventure, whether it’s a weekend road trip or something new to explore nearby.
